## Known Support Issues

- Some unresolved cases (battery defects, lens issues)
- Support can be slow during high-volume periods
- Priority assistance available via Discord staff contact

---

## Tips for Getting Help

1. **Be specific** about your issue with photos/videos if applicable
2. **Check Discord** first - your issue may already have a solution
3. **Try community workarounds** while waiting for official support
4. **Document everything** - keep receipts, order numbers, correspondence
