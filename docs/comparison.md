## vs. XReal One/Pro

| Aspect | INMO Air 3 | XReal One/Pro |
|--------|------------|---------------|
| **Resolution** | 1080p | 1080p |
| **Standalone** | Yes | No (needs device) |
| **Audio** | Tinny speakers | Bose (excellent) |
| **FOV** | 36° | 52-58° |
| **Weight** | ~140g | Similar |
| **Price** | $1000-$1299 | $680 |
| **Prescription Support** | Yes | Limited |
| **Use Case** | Productivity | Media consumption |

### Summary

- **Choose INMO** for standalone computing and productivity
- **Choose XReal** for media consumption with better FOV and audio

---

## vs. RayNeo X2/X3 Pro

| Aspect | INMO Air 3 | RayNeo X2/X3 Pro |
|--------|------------|------------------|
| **Display** | Full color, centered | X2: Monochrome, slanted; X3 Pro: Color |
| **OS** | Full Android, open ecosystem | Locked down |
| **Battery** | 1.5-2 hours | X3 Pro: 30 minutes |
| **Form Factor** | Standard glasses | Better/slimmer |

### Summary

- **Choose INMO** for full Android experience and longer battery
- **Choose RayNeo** for better form factor (with trade-offs)

---

## vs. Rokid Glasses

| Aspect | INMO Air 3 | Rokid |
|--------|------------|-------|
| **Display** | Full color | Monochrome |
| **Purpose** | All-day computing | Navigation/translation |
| **Weight** | Heavier | Lightweight, all-day wearable |
| **Price** | Higher | Cheaper |
| **Optics** | Waveguide | Birdbath |

### Summary

- **Choose INMO** for full productivity computing
- **Choose Rokid** for lightweight, simple navigation/translation use

---

## vs. Meta Ray-Ban Display

| Aspect | INMO Air 3 | Meta Ray-Ban |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| **Capability** | Full computer in glasses | Limited AI, notifications |
| **Form Factor** | Heavier, more obvious | Lighter, looks like regular glasses |
| **Extended Use** | Better for long sessions | Better for quick glances |
| **Ecosystem** | Open Android | Meta ecosystem |

### Summary

- **Choose INMO** for serious productivity and computing
- **Choose Meta** for casual, everyday smart features with better looks
